OBJECTIVE

This organization matches prospective adopters with cats in need of loving homes. Ensuring that every cat has a secure, caring, and home, along with encouraging responsible pet ownership.

  • Awareness about the benefits of adopting cats from shelters and rescue organizations.

  • Organize regular community events, such as adoption fairs, pet care workshops, and fundraising activities.

  • Taking care of a cat helps owners develop empathy and compassion by teaching them how to recognise and react to the needs and behaviours of their pet.
