PROCESS OF ADOPTION:

  • Step 1: Read

    Begin by reading the Why Adopt? section to discover more about the positive aspects and responsibilities of adopting a cat. This will give important context for understanding why adoption is a good decision.

  • Step 2: Decide

    Visit our "Cat Adoption" page where we have listed various cats available for adoption. Each listing includes the cat's code, name, age, weight, color, personality, and other vital information. Take your time looking through the profiles and selecting the cat that best suits your demands and preferences. Once you have decided, select the option by checking the box in the Register section.

  • Step 3: Register

    Continue to the Register page to finish the adoption process. Fill out all of the essential forms, including your personal information, contact information, and any other information needed for a successful adoption process. Verify the correctness of your information one more before submitting the form.
